About Us:

Fusion Fest is not just the annual cultural fest of NMIMS University but a kaleidoscope of emotions, memories, and boundless creativity. This 'Chaar Din ka Sapna' is the place where people experience four power-packed days of passionate competitions, fun games and activities, events of renowned artists, electrifying concerts, soothing nightlife, and many more. All in all, MI is a testament to the indomitable spirit of exploration and self-expression, etching lasting memories in the hearts of all who partake in its magic.

Theme:The Retro Space

The retro space theme is a nostalgic homage to the futuristic visions of the mid-20th century. It captures the essence of classic science fiction and space exploration, drawing inspiration from retro-futuristic aesthetics, vibrant colors, and imaginative spacecraft designs. This theme often incorporates elements such as vintage rocket ships, futuristic cityscapes, celestial bodies, and retro-inspired typography to evoke a sense of wonder and adventure reminiscent of the space age era. With its blend of nostalgia and imagination, the retro space theme invites us to embark on a journey through time and space, where the possibilities are as boundless as the universe itself.
